Title: Repository sepek moved to git.exherbo.org
Author: Paul Seidler <sepek@exherbo.org>
Content-Type: text/plain
Posted: 2011-12-06
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 1.0

::sepek was moved to git.exherbo.org.

To receive further updates from this repository you need to change the
'sync' field in the repository config file, which is usually located at

    /etc/paludis/repositories/sepek.conf

to "git://git.exherbo.org/dev/sepek.git".
